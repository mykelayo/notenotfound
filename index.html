notenotfound: Self-Destructing Encrypted Notes

Share secrets safely with links that self-destruct after one read

notenotfound is a free, open-source tool for sharing sensitive information. Write a private note, get a unique link, share it. The moment the recipient reads the note, it is permanently deleted. Gone forever, by design.

AES-256-GCM encryption, bank-grade security

Your note is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it is stored. We cannot read your notes. Notes auto-expire after 1 hour, 24 hours, 3 days, or 7 days even if never read.

Free Privnote alternative and OnetimeSecret alternative

notenotfound is free, open source, and has no sign-up requirement. Use it to share passwords, API keys, private messages, and temporary credentials.
